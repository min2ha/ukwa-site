# Pharmacy Research Wales 

Pharmacy Research Wales is a strategic action plan to build pharmacy research and research capacity for pharmacy professionals in Wales. The 5 year strategic plan for pharmacy research in Wales is led by the All Wales Chief Pharmacists Committee.
