# North Craven Heritage Trust

The North Craven Heritage Trust was formed to foster interest in and care for the distinctive character and heritage of North Craven by encouraging high standards of planning   and architecture; stimulating public interest in and care for   the beauty, character and heritage of the area; encouraging the preservation, protection and sympathetic development of features of historic or public interest.
