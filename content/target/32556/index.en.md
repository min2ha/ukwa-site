# Wales Institute of Social & Economic Research, Data & Methods (WISERD)

WISERD has been designated by the Welsh Government as a National Research Centre. The centre was established in 2008 and is based within five Universities in Wales: Aberystwyth, Bangor, Cardiff, South Wales and Swansea. The Wales Institute of Social and Economic Research, Data & Methods (WISERD) and draws together social science researchers from a number of disciplines including sociology, economics, geography and political science.
