# TickIT

TickIT is an independent organisation set up to educate and encourage people from the African, Caribbean and African and Caribbean heritage communities to register and vote. Their primary activities include working directly and through our networks to reach non voters and holding meetings and workshops in various community settings to emphasise the importance of voting.
