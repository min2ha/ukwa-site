# Rhiannon Lucy Cosslett

Rhiannon Lucy Cosslett is a columnist, feature writer and editor for the Guardian newspaper, contributing mainly to the opinion and features sections, and the Weekend magazine. She also co-founded The Vagenda, a feminist blog which was published in book form by Vintage and her first novel, The Tyranny of Lost Things, which was inspired by the London squatting movement, will be published by Sandstone Press in Summer 2018.
