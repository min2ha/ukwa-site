# Llangynwyd Village Hall

Llangynwyd Village Hall was built as a schoolhouse by the Margam Estate during the 19th Century and used until the 1920s. It then became a church dedicated to St. Stephen until dwindling church attendance during the 1990s resulted in it being sold for use as a studio for the renowned Canadian born artist William McClure Brown. It hall now been restored and is owned by Llangynwyd Community Assoctaion.
