# NHS Wales: Wales Information Governance Board (WIGB)

According to the site, 'The Wales Information Governance Board (WIGB) is a high level, independent, non statutory forum which was set up by the Minister of Health and Social Care for Wales. WIGB provides information governance and assurance advice on 'direction' and 'standards' of information governance policy in connection with the work that is undertaken relating to privacy and sharing of information across Wales and provides assurance to the Minister of Health and Social Care for Wales that these standards are being met'.
