Phil Rowlands is a former working actor and is now a freelance writer and film producer. He has written feature films, TV and radio dramas, documentaries and animation series and worked as a script doctor. He is also the founder and Managing Director of Funky Medics,  a production company focussing on innocative health education and is an Honorary Associate of Cardiff University. Phil Rowlands is originally from Haverfordwest and now lives in Penarth, Cardiff.
