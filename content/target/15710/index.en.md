# British Cardiac Research Trust (BCRT), The

<p>The British Cardiac Research Trust (BCRT) was launched in February 2003 as a charitable organisation dedicated to cardiovascular research. The charity supports research into all aspects of heart disease through the work of scientists, doctors and nurses.</p>
