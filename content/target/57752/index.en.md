# Ethnicity Facts and Figures

In the UK, 87% of people are White, and 13% belong to a Black, Asian, Mixed or Other ethnic group. You can use this service to find information about the different experiences of people from a variety of ethnic backgrounds. It gathers data collected by government in one place, making it available to the public, specialists and charities.
