# Cardiff County and Vale of Glamorgan Music Service | Sir Caerdydd Gwasanaeth Cerdd Bro Morgannwg

Cardiff County and Vale of Glamorgan Music Service is a non-profit making joint-Council music service supporting schools to deliver high quality music education. It provides the opportunity for young musicians to participate in music activities across Cardiff and the Vale of Glamorgan.
