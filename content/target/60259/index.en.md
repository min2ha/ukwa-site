# Welsh Economic Review

The Welsh Economic Review is published by Cardiff University Press and provides authoritative and objective analysis and commentary on the Welsh economy. The Welsh Economic Review was first published from 1988 until 2013. It was relaunched in 2016 and is now published by Cardiff University Press.
