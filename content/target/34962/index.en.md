# Coalition of Aid and Development Agencies in Northern Ireland (CADA)

The Coalition of Aid and Development Agencies in Northern Ireland (CADA) is an umbrella organisation of those overseas aid agencies with an active presence in Northern Ireland. There are currently 29 member agencies, which, are collectively supported by more than 150,000 people across Northern Ireland who donate their money and time to campaign, fund-raise and volunteer.
