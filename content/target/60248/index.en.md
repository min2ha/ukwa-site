# Carl Chabble

Carl Chapple is a member of the RCA (Royal Cambrian Academy) and his work is held in private collections across the UK and internationally, and is represented in the permanent collection of the Museum of Modern Art Cymru. His studio is based in Barry. He is primarily interested in painting people.
