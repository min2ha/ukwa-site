# Ghazalaw

Ghazalaw is a collaboration of Indian Ghazal and Welsh folk tradition and involves Mumbai singer Tauseef Akhtar and Welsh singer-songwriter Gwyneth Glyn together to the accompaniment of harp, tabla, guitar and harmonium. They are supported by the Arts Council of Wales and sponsored by Air India.
