# Saga

Saga is a British company focused on serving the needs of those aged 50 and over. Saga Holidays provides package holidays and tours across the globe. Saga Services provides a wide range of Insurance products - Motor, Home, Travel, Caravan, Commercial Van, Pet, Private Medical, Life Insurance, Motorhome and legal services.
