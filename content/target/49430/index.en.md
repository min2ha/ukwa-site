# Etcetera Theatre

The Etcetera is a pub theatre established in 1986 in the London Borough of Camden. It hosts and supports new fringe theatre, comedy, cabaret and musical acts. It is also a founding venue of the Camden Fringe, home of the London Horror Festival, creator of the Black Box Festival, and hosts of an Edinburgh Fringe preview season.
