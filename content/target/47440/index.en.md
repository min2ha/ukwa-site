# Madness of North Wales, The:  The History of Mental Health in North Wales

Over the past 15 years, the Department of Psychological Medicine have collected a huge amount of quantative data on patients from North West Wales going through psychiatric unit (Hergest) at Ysbyty Gwynedd (the main District Hospital in NW Wales) and are charting a portrait of diseases rising and falling during the period 1995 to 2010 in the area.
