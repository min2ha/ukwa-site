# Stars Appeal: Salisbury District Hospital’s charity

The Stars Appeal is Salisbury District Hospital’s charity (no.1052284). They raise money for patients and their families by funding extra care and equipment above that provided by the NHS. They help local people across all wards and departments, with a particular focus on cancer, cardiac care and children.
