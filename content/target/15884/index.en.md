# Waterloo 200

'Waterloo 200 1815-2015 A Defining Moment in European History' website is run by Waterloo 200 Ltd, which is an umbrella organisation to oversee the celebration of the 200th anniversary of the Battle of Waterloo. In 2009 Waterloo 200 Ltd was granted charitable status and it is linked with The Waterloo Committee founded by the Duke of Wellington.
