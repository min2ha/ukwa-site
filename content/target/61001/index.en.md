# Harry Robertson Painter

Harry Robertson is a painter from Scarborough who lives and works in North Wales. His works draws upon early Flemish and 19th Century European landscape painting. He is an elected member of the Royal Cambrian Academy of Art (RCA) and much his work is of the Welsh landscape.
