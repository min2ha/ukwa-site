# YMCA Scotland

Site of a Christian youth organisation, open to people of all faiths and none and we work to transform the lives of children and young people, empowering them to create positive change in their lives and communities. We seek to work with all children and young people in an non-judgemental and supportive manner. Also known as Young Men's Christian Association.
