# Friends of St Michael’s Church, Betws-y-Coed

Friends of St Michael’s was formed, to raise both awareness of the building, and money to preserve St Michael's Church in Betws-y-Coed. The church closed in 2000, and the building is now leased from the Church in Wales and run by the Friends.
