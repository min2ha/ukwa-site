# Janis Fry Art

Janis Fry is a specialist in ancient yew trees and a published author and writer on this subject and the yew and the mythology of the Tree of Life. Her paintings are in acrylic, watercolour, ink and gouache, and mixed media. She is a member and Vice President of the Royal Watercolour Society of Wales.
