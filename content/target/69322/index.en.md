Lipstick and Chainsaws is an Arts Council of Wales and First Campus funded project delivered by Winding Snake Productions throughout 2016. Over the course of the project 60 pupils took part in workshops covering different disciplines with professional artists and included a guided explorations of exhibitions, facilities and collections based at The National Museums and Galleries of Wales, G39, Chapter Gallery and the University of South Wales.
