The Council of British Hajjis is a not-for-profit charity whose aims are to work for the welfare of British Hajjis (pilgrims) and to ensure that they are relieved of any distress and to ensure that Hajj service providers perform their obligations towards the pilgrims making the experience of Hajj & Umrah an enjoyable and positive one.
