# AgorIP

"AgorIP can help you realise the potential of your idea, 
product, service or research as their team of experts based at Swansea University can help you take your innovation to the marketplace and help make it a commercial success. AgorIP is funded by the following organisations: Welsh Government, Swansea University and the Europan Regional Development Fund. The inventions must assist West Wales, North Wales or the Valleys."
