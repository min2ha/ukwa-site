Ewenny Priory is in Ewenny, Vale of Glamorgan. It was a monastery of the Benedictine order, founded in the 12th century and unusual in that it had military-style defences and regarded as one of the finest fortified religious buildings in Britain. It is also one of the best-preserved example of a Norman priory church in South Wales. The church is a Grade I listed building, the house is Grade II and now and Event and Wedding Venue.
