# Information Literacy

This site is run by information professionals from key UK organisations actively involved in the field of information literacy and who are members of the CILIP Literacy Group. The site supports practitioners and researchers from around the world with an interest in information and digital literacy by providing news, case studies, examples of best practice and freely available toolkits.
