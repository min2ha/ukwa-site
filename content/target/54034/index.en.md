# Workers Revolutionary Party 

The Workers Revolutionary Party are the British section of the International Committee of the Fourth International, founded by Leon Trotsky. The Workers Revolutionary Party fights to build a leadership throughout the working class and youth to lead the struggle for the British and world socialist revolutions. The youth section of the WRP, the Young Socialists, organises young workers and students in the struggle for socialism and a future. They produce a daily paper, the News Line and a weekly youth paper Young Socialist.
