A website focusing on the English poet, historical novelist, critic, and classicist, Robert Graves (1895 – 1985). He enlisted as a junior officer in the Royal Welch Fusiliers wrote behind the lines in World War One. He later became known as one of the most important English language 'Love' poets. The website is designed to help researchers and contains biographic and bibliographic material and many links to external sources. The resource is the responsibility of  the Robert Graves Trust, St John’s College, Oxford.
