# Tabernacle Congregational Church, Haverfordwest

Tabernacle Congregational Church is in Haverfordwest. The Congregational Federation in Wales is a Christian Denomination whose Churches use the English language for worship. They have a friendly relationship with the Welsh-speaking Congregational Denomination, Undeb yr Annibynwyr Cymraeg.
