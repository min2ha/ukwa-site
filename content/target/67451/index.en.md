# Hi-ProfILE : Health Informatics Professional Identity, Leadership and Education

The HI-ProfILE contacts database brings together Health Informatics professionals in Wales and is open to all NHS staff in Health Informatics roles.  It is used to locate colleagues in various specialties of Health Informatics in NHS Wales and HI-ProfILE will use the database to bring you up-to-date news in the Health Informatics professional arena.
