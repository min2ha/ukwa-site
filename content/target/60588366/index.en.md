# British Sociological Association (BSA)

The British Sociological Association (BSA) is the national subject association for sociologists in the UK and its primary objective is to promote sociology.  Members of the association come from a wide range of backgrounds and include researchers, teachers, students and practitioners in a variety of fields. It was founded in 1951 and is a registered charitable company.
