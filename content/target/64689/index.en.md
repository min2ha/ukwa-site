# Mail on Sunday, The: Bang goes Brexit if we fall for this customs con trick: Former minister DAVID JONES on how the EU is 'trying to bully us into a bad deal'  

Jones explains in his Mail on Sunday article that the scheme devised by the Prime Minister's Chief Brexit Adviser, Olly Robbins, would mean the UK could indeed, as the Leave side promised in the referendum campaign, be free to set our own, lower tariffs on imports. But, apparently to avoid a hard Irish border, ignoring the technological solution proposal, the UK would take on the role of the EU’s tax collector. The scheme would mean the UK imposing the full tariffs on products destined for the Continent and passing the money on to Brussels. Importers of goods staying in the UK would receive a rebate.
