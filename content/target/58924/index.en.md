Galata Coins was established in 1971 and sold ancient Greek, Roman and Byzantine coins soon incorporating the coin dealing activities of Paul Withers which included hammered coins, world coins, tokens and medals. Galata is now based in Llanfyllin, Powys and now also publish Numismatic Books. Paul Withers is a fellow of the Royal Numismatic Society.
