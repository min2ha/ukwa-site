# CLIC Sargent for Children with Cancer: UK General Election 2015

CLIC Sargent recognises that every child or young person with cancer has a different experience. A cancer diagnosis also affects the whole family in many different ways, so we take time to listen and understand. Their aim is to help ensure that the whole family receives the support which works best for them.
