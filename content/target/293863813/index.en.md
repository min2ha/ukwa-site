# Ymchwil iechyd a Gofal Cymru | Health and Care Research Wales

Health and Care Research Wales is funded and overseen by the Welsh Government. It provides an 'infrastructure to support and increase capacity in research and development (R&D), runs a range of responsive funding schemes and manages the NHS R&D funding allocation'.
