# Association of Pakistani Physicians and Surgeons of the United Kingdom (APPSUK)

The APPS is an independent, educational and not for profit organisation with no political motives. It is a professional association that provides a platform for Pakistani-British doctors, dentists & allied health professionals in the United Kingdom as well as those coming from Pakistan.
