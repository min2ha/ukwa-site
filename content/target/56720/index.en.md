# Vertical Dance Kate Lawrence

Vertical Dance Kate Lawrence is a theatrical producers located in Caernarfon. According to the site, 'Vertical dance is an exciting, emerging form of dance that typically uses rock climbing equipment (ropes, harnesses and abseil devices) to suspend dancers off the ground on a range of vertical surfaces. It takes place in a variety of locations, from theatres and their foyers to the outside of buildings and rock faces.
