# Welsh Singers Competition | Cystadleuaeth i Gantorian Cymru

The Welsh Singers Competition is open to singers aged 18 to 32 born or raised in Wales and received a significant proportion of their education in Wales. The competition is arranged by Live Music Now in association with BBC Cymru Wales and with the support of The Arts Council of Wales.
