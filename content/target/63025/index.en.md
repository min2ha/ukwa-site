# Medical Protection Society Limited (MPS)

The Medical Protection Society Limited (“MPS”) is a protection organisation for doctors, dentists and healthcare professionals. Membership provides access to expert advice and support together with the right to request indemnity for complaints or claims arising from professional practice.
