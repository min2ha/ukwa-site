# Origin Scotland

Site of an evangelistic ministry based in Edinburgh, Scotland, dedicated to reaching people with the Gospel of Jesus Christ, and to promoting unity in the church through praise and worship events as well as being a promoter and organiser of Christian events, providing services to churches in Edinburgh and beyond in worship events, praise events, outreach events and event consultancy.
