# Western Association of Ballet Schools

Nine dancing schools from Bournemouth, Poole, Southbourne and Christchurch are at present affiliated and the record is an impressive, if not unprecedented one, an extensive repertoire of full scale choreographic and dramatic presentations of notable originality have been produced so far, involving some five thousand or so young people to date.
