# Penterry Online

<p>Formed in 2001, the Penterry Church &amp; Field Restoration Committee aims to ensure that St. Mary's Church continues to act as a focal point for the Penterry community for years to come. St. Mary's Church, Penterry to the North of Chepstow. Once the parish church for a community believed to have been wiped out by the plague, St. Mary's fell into disrepair before being restored in Victorian days.</p>
