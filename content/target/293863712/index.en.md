# Early Support Wales

'The Early Support programme was a Welsh Government programme developed to improve the lives of children with additional needs and their families, and was specifically aimed at children aged 5 years or under. Children in Wales led a partnership to deliver the Early Support programme across Wales with 4-year with funding from Welsh Government. The funding for the Early Support programme ended in March 2013'. - home page.
