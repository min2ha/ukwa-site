# ManKind Initiative Blog

The ManKind Initiative  (www.mankind.org.uk) is a national charity that provides help and support for male victims of domestic abuse and domestic violence. Our vision and mission are below.

This blog is aimed at providing a forum  for the domestic abuse sector to report, debate and discuss the latest development in support for male victims of domestic abuse in the UK, a crime that has a damaging effect 0n 720,000 men every year.
